About Us

Find more about us. Welcome to Narvana Restaurant, where passion for food meets exceptional service. Founded in 2017, our restaurant has been a staple in the Kumasi community, known for its warm ambiance and delectable dishes. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience through quality ingredients, creative menus, and outstanding customer service. Our Story Narvana Restaurant was established by Mrs. Lorinda Djifa with a vision to create a space where people can come together to enjoy great food and company. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, but our commitment to excellence remains the same. Our Values Quality: We use only the finest ingredients. Creativity: We strive to innovate and surprise with our dishes. Community: We are proud to be a part of the Kumasi community and support local initiatives.
